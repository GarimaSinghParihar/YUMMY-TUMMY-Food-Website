Khandvi

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breasts cut into ½ inch pieces 400 grams
  • Red chilli paste 1 teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Ginger-garlic paste 1 tablespoon
  • Yogurt 4 tablespoons
  • Garam masala powder ½ teaspoon
  • Lemon 1
  • Extra virgin olive oil 6 tablespoons
  • Orange juice 1 cup
  • Green capsicums cut into ½ inch pieces 2 medium
  • Cooked rice 2 cups
  • Black pepper powder ½ teaspoon
  • Sugar 1 teaspoon
  • Butter 2 tablespoons

Method

  1. Marinate chicken pieces with red chilli paste, salt, ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, garam masala powder and juice of lemon and mix.
  2. Add 2 tbsps extra virgin olive oil and mix and marinate for a few hours in the refrigerator.
  3. Heat a non stick pan and pour orange juice into it. Add salt, pepper powder and sugar to the orange juice and cook.
  4. When the sauce thickens a little, take it off the heat. Add butter and mix well.
  5. String the chicken pieces and capsicum pieces alternately onto skewers. Heat a non stick tawa, drizzle a little oil and place the skewers on it
    6. .
  6. Turn the skewers so that the chicken gets cooked evenly all around.
  7. Pack rice into a mould, demould it on a serving plate and drizzle 1 tbsp extra virgin oil over it.
  8. Place the skewers over the rice, drizzle the sauce over the chicken and rice and serve immediately.
  9. Use up the remaining chicken and rice similarly.
